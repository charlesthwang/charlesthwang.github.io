About Me

Hi, I'm Charles

—a UI/UX designer and developer based in Washington, D.C. Previously, I was a web developer at U.Group (formerly CHIEF), where I collaborated with designers, developers, strategists, and product managers to bring digital concepts to life.

And while I enjoyed development, I also increasingly became interested in the why behind the work I was doing and how people use products — the identification and prioritization of problems and how to best solve them.

I'm particularly interested in using learnings in psychology and human behavior to take on usability challenges.

I love to learn and often end up in deep dives on various design topics (and other random things).
