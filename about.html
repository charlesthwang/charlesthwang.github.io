About Me

Hi, I'm Charles

—a self-taught UI/UX designer based in Washington, D.C. Previously, I was a web developer at U.Group (formerly CHIEF), where I collaborated with strategists, managers, designers, and other developers to bring digital concepts to life.

And as much as I enjoyed development, I wanted to be more involved in the why behind the work I was doing—the identification and prioritization of problems and how to best solve them.

I'm particularly interested in interaction design, using learnings in psychology and human behavior to take on usability challenges.

I love to learn and often end up in deep dives on various design topics (and other random things).
